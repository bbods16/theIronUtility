# Method Overrides

This document tracks approved modifications to previously committed methods, including their rationale and hash before/after the change.

## Format:

### `[File Path]`

#### `[Method Name]`

*   **Rationale**: [Explanation for why the method was modified instead of extended.]
*   **Before Hash**: [SHA256 hash of the method's body before modification.]
*   **After Hash**: [SHA256 hash of the method's body after modification.]
*   **Commit**: [Link to the commit where the modification was applied.]
*   **Authorized By**: [User/Agent who authorized the `UPDATE_OLD_METHOD`/`MODIFY_EXISTING` flag.]
